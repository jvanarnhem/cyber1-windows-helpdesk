1. Investigation & Research

Open a new tab and Google the exact error code from above. What is the system actually complaining about? (e.g., "Not enough disk space").
If you had this computer in front of you, where would you go? Example: "Settings > Windows Update" or "Start Menu > System Information".

2. Permissions & Security

Look at the user's role in the Context box. Do they have the right to install software or change system files? Why or why not?
What happens if we ignore this ticket? Example: "If we don't apply this patch, the computer is vulnerable to known malware."
